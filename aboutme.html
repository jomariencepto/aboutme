About

I'm Jomari — a full stack developer who likes owning a project end to end: the screen a user sees, the logic that runs behind it, the database holding it together, and the infrastructure that keeps it online.

My recent work spans point-of-sale and billing systems for small businesses and AI-assisted academic tooling — different problems, same approach: understand the real workflow first, then build something that actually fits it.

Outside the codebase, I'm comfortable getting hands-on with servers, deployments, and basic network diagnostics — because "it works on my machine" isn't good enough.
